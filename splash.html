In this door-themed splash page example: The splash page is designed to resemble a closed door. There's a doorknob (door-handle) and a sign (door-sign) on the door. A welcoming message is displayed, inviting visitors to "Open the door to a world of discovery!". An "Enter" button encourages visitors to proceed to the main content of your website. You can style the door, doorknob, sign, and text to match your website's theme and branding.
